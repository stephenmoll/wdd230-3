Site Purpose

The purpose of the Unofficial Geobots Fanhub Website is to make it easier for new fans to get into the popular Lego Geobots theme and to bring fans of the theme closer together as a community of builders and adventurers. We also support newer fans to the Geobots universe by introducing characters and storylines that are fun and easy to get into. Whether you are a normal person who is interested in Geobots, a parent trying to get a present fo their child, or a Geobots Super-ultra-megafan, this is the place for you!
